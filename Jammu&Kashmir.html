Jammu & Kashmir:

Jammu and Kashmir is a region administered by India as a union territory
and consisting of the southern portion of the larger Kashmir region,
which has been the subject of a dispute between India and Pakistan since 1947,
and between India and China since 1962. The Line of Control separates Jammu and Kashmir
from the Pakistani-administered territories of Azad Kashmir and Gilgit-Baltistan in the west and north.
